How to use Edpuzzle in Classroom

By Houda Bou Rafeh

Step 1: Create an Account
  1. To start using Edpuzzle in classroom, you should first have an account.Click on Sign Up button.

  2. Click on the I'm a Teacher button to be able to have your class and create your own content.

  3. After signing up, log in using your email address and password.


Step 2: Add Content

You can now create your own content to use it in the classroom. Click on Add Content button.

You can notice that you have 4 different options; "Discover Video Content" where you can use videos from external sites, "Upload Video" from your computer or Google Drive, "Record Video", and "Student Project".

To add a YouTube video to your content,Click on Discover Video Content.


Step 3: Choose a YouTube Video

After clicking on "Discover Video Content", Discover page will open.

There are 3 different sections; Community, My Network, YouTube.

Click on YouTube to find a video.


Step 4: Edit the Video
  • Write any topic you would like to find in the search toolbar.

  • Write for example "Html".

  • Choose the video that you need.

    • warningAfter choosing the video, you are able to edit it by clicking on the edit button on the right of the page. You can cut the video, add voiceover and questions.


  • When done editing, click on Finish button.

Step 5: Share the Edited Video

When editing the video is done, click on "Share Preview" button to share and embed its link.

This edited video is now added to My Content and ready to use in the classroom.
